My Story

I am currently living in Colorado Springs, but am originally from Chicago. I love to travel and see new places, one of my favorite travels being France. Being outdoors in general is my favorite past time, hiking, swimming, or snowboarding, I am happiest.

Along with traveling and making my way through new places I love working through new projects. I have a self-help podcast that I work on in my free time. It gives me creative freedom and each episode allows me to learn more and more about varied topics. I want to continue learning through whatever I am doing.

I have worked in the restaurant industry for 8 years starting as a hostess at a Spanish Tapas restaurant making my way to Assistant General Manger for a breakfast eatery as an integral part of the opening process for the restaurant. I was lucky enough to be able to train our new front of house staff and create all of our training materials and documentation related to our entire staff. I loved making our training presentations going over our new menu, techniques relating to barista art, and steps of service.
